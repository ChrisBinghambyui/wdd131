SOLARIRUM FRENCH - 500 Common Word Translations (REVISED)
Fantasy Language based on SolarirumFrench name patterns
Linguistic roots: Old French / Langue d'oïl (11th–13th century)
Words are evolved and stylized from Old French — not modern French directly.

KEY SOURCE WORDS (Old French):
  le/la/les (the), un/une (a), estre (to be), avoir (to have), faire (to do/make),
  dire (to say), veoir (to see), aler (to go), venir (to come), savoir (to know),
  vouloir (to want), donner (to give), tenir (to hold), sentir (to feel),
  homme (man), femme (woman), enfant (child), roi (king), peuple (people),
  terre (earth), feu (fire), eau (water), ciel (sky), nuit (night), jour (day),
  grant (great), bel (beautiful), bon (good), vrai (true), fort (strong)

NOTE: Old French phonology differs significantly from modern French:
  - Final consonants were pronounced
  - Many words ended in -er, -ier, -oir, -el, -al, -ain
  - Diphthongs were common: -ieu, -eau, -ai, -oi, -ui
  - Words like "estre" (être), "veoir" (voir), "savoir" retain archaic -re/-oir endings

PHONETIC GUIDE FOR SOLARIRUM FRENCH
===================================================================================

Old French sounds preserved in Solarirum:
  oï  = diphthong (as in OF "oïl" — the origin of "oui")
  ieu = triphthong (as in OF "lieu")
  ei  = diphthong (as in OF "rei" = king)
  eü  = rounded front vowel (as in OF "feü" = fire)
  ïe  = falling diphthong, two syllables

Stylization rules for Solarirum French:
  -vel    = nominal suffix (all nouns take this ending)
  -iel    = adverbial/particle suffix (softer)
  -el     = pronoun/article suffix
  -ïel    = emphatic suffix (stressed form)
  eïr     = verb infinitive ending (replaces OF -er/-ir/-re)
  Final consonants: always pronounced (unlike modern French)
  Nasals: -ain, -ien, -on are fully nasalized

Prefix patterns from the name list:
  aur-   = golden/radiant (Aur-Solind, Aur-Miravel)
  bel-   = beautiful (Bel-Auriel, Bel-Lirond)
  cel-   = sky/high (Cel-Auriel, Cel-Lirond)
  mir-   = reflected/seen (Miravel, Mir-Aurond)
  cal-   = calling/summoning (Cal-Auriel, Calvoire)
  syl-   = forest/growing (Sylvaine, Sylvariel)
  lir-   = lyrical/singing (Lirindel, Lirond)
  vel-   = flowing/veiled (Velindre, Vel-Aurind)
  sel-   = chosen/self (Selveth, Sel-Auriel)
