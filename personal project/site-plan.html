Overview

Purpose

I'm writing a TTRPG rulebook for a personal project, and I've made a couple games that I've ported over to html, so for this project I want to make a site that will host some of the info and rules for the ttrpg. The main page will be a landing/welcome page, with a child page for building a character. Another page will be for spellcrafting, a main component of my game.

Audience

It's going to be a portfolio piece, so potential hirers. Also, fellow nerds who want to try my game out.

Dynamic elements

The character builder will take you through the process step by step, and then I have an in-depth feat system on level up that I will use to have only the feats that the player has the prerequisites for appearing.

Content

Home page

Welcome to the official website for Scrolls and Steel, a new TTRPG loosely based on D&D and The Elder Scrolls. If you're looking for a system with tactical combat and freedom of customization, you're in the right place.

WHAT SETS SCROLLS & STEEL APART

Scrolls & Steel was designed to be a classless system with complete freedom of choice. A knight who practices magic will eventually be able to keep up with the mages they encounter. A thief who practices enchanting will be able to create wonderous items of their own before long.

The robust spellcrafting and alchemy systems allow for players to make wise use of their downtime. The structured capabilities included in this starter ruleset allow practiced mages to create spells that fulfill the precise niche they need them to. Everything from a high speed flight spell to a massive area-of-effect silencing spell is able to be made and customized.

Try out the custom character creator to create an adventurer of your own to brave the dangers of Aethermoor. Choose a pre-defined archetype, or build from the ground up as you craft the hero (or villain) who will play a major role in the coming times of strife and war, one way or another. Will you be an alchemist, brewing bubbling mixtures of weal and woe, or a paladin, wielding sword, shield, and spells to protect those they've sworn themselves to? Will your character be one of the many diverse human lineages, or perhaps something stranger, like one the reclusive rat-men known as Naukin or the minotaur-like Bovari?
